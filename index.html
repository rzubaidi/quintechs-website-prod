Our Products

Innovative solutions built on Microsoft Dynamics 365.


MEA HR and Payroll

MEA HR and Payroll is a comprehensive solution for Microsoft Dynamics 365 that simplifies HR and payroll management across the Middle East and GCC region. It automates salary processing, attendance tracking, and compliance with regional labor and tax laws, giving organizations efficiency, accuracy, and control over workforce operations.

Learn more

Jordan Tax Connector

We provide a custom solution based on Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations to seamlessly sync e-invoices with the Jordan Income and Sales Tax Department (ISTD). This solution ensures full compliance with local regulations, including the automatic generation and integration of required QR codes for all invoices.
